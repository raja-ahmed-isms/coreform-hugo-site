Support packages

We're committed to helping you be successful.

Coreform will be there to ensure your success. Coreform offers complimentary support during the trial period, and standard and premium support packages after you buy.

Complimentary support.

With all Coreform products, including trial licenses, you receive complimentary technical support for the first 30 days. Complimentary technical support is unlimited email support for installation, launch, or re-installation of the software. Complimentary support does not include meshing support or training. After the first 30 days, Standard Technical Support is available to customers who purchase an annual lease license or who participate in the Coreform Software Maintenance Program. Premium technical support is available for an additional fee.

Standard technical support.

Standard technical support provides help to solve technical problems with the software. Standard support does not include training, customization, or meshing services.

Standard support includes the following support benefits:

  • Email support (support@coreform.com)
  • 72 hour response time, Monday-Friday 8:30AM-5:00PM MST (except U.S. holidays)
  • Forum and online support
  • Single point of contact from your company

Contact Coreform for more information about our Standard Technical Support package.
