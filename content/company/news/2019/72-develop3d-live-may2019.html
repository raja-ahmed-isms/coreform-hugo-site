Coreform presented on the main stage of Develop3D Live on April 15, 2019. Presenting alongside senior leaders from Ansys, SolidWorks, OnShape, Altair, Autodesk, PTC, and others at this premier industry event, Coreform CEO Matt Sederberg shared Coreform's advances in highly accurate smooth spline-based simulation software.

He also gave the first glimpses into Coreform Process and Coreform Analyze, the preprocessor and solver, respectively, that Coreform is developing based on smooth splines. A recording of the presentation is available on the recorded livestream from Develop3D Live. His talk begins at the 4:20:00 mark. In the presentation, Matt shares examples of how simulation based on smooth splines promises better accuracy in less time, increased robustness, lower simulation costs, and minimal change to workflows.
