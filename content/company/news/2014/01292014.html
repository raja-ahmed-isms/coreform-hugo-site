AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, January 29, 2014 — csimsoft, a leading developer of advanced meshing tools for the CFD and FEA, announced today that it will release the next version of its Trelis software with the Reprise License Manager from Reprise Software, Inc.

"RLM simple has what our company needs in a license manager. It has to protect our intellectual property without making the installation process difficult for our customers," said Randy Morris, csimsoft's Managing Director. "Its robust set of features and ease-of-use made the choice very easy."

RLM provides great flexibility in software licensing to Independent Software Vendors while simultaneously protecting their rights.

About Reprise Software, Inc.

Reprise Software is a self-funded, growing, and profitable software license management company. Since 2006, the company has been leading the way in the software licensing field. Reprise has continued to make a name for themselves by offering an exceptional product and superior customer support. For more information about the RLM program visit www.RepriseSoftware.com

About csimsoft

csimsoft is a leading developer of pre- and post-processing solutions for the computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis communities. Delivering exceptional performance and outstanding value, csimsoft's award winning products are used every day by engineers in many of the worlds largest organizations. csimsoft was founded in 2004 to commercialize Sandia National Laboratory's CUBIT software. csimsoft is headquartered in Utah, U.S.A.
