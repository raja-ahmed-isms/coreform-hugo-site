AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, July 19, 2012 — csimsoft announced today the release of Cubit 13.2, its latest version of the Cubit pre-processor for generating high-quality tetrahedral and hexahedral meshes for the CFD and FEA communities.

The latest version of Cubit includes improved meshing and geometry features, a new 64-bit tet mesher, and improved third-party format support. "The new features in 13.2 demonstrate our responsiveness to our customers' needs," says Paul Ressler, Director of Sales and Marketing. "It represents our on-going commitment to provide superior meshing tools to the worldwide community of engineering professions." Included in the list of new features are:

Remesh Tet Command � The new remesh tet command can replace deformed or poor quality tetrahedra after an analysis where some portion of the mesh is deformed.

64-bit Tet Mesher � The new 64-bit tet mesher that allows for significantly larger meshes than Cubit's previous tet mesher.

Defeaturing Power Tool � This new power tool reduces the defeaturing of solid models to a single button click or command.

For a complete list of new features in Cubit 13.2, visit csimsoft.com.
