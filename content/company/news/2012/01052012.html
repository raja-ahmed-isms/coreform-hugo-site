AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, January 5, 2012 — Computational Simulation Software, LLC (csimsoft), a leader in pre-processor technology, today announced maintenance amnesty for all previous versions of Cubit prior to March 1, 2012. All previous Cubit licenses may be upgraded to the recently released Cubit 13.1 for the cost of a single year of maintenance.

"The amnesty allows those with older licenses of Cubit to get version 13.1 no matter how much back maintenance is owed," commented Paul Ressler, Director of Sales and Marketing for csimsoft. "This decision is in keeping with our customer service record and commitment to increasing the use of Cubit."

For more information, contact your sales representative or call (801) 717-2296 ext 7.

About csimsoft

csimsoft is a leading developer of pre- and post-processing solutions for the computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis communities. csimsoft was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in American Fork, Utah.
