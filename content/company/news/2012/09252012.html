AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, September 25, 2012 — csimsoft has released a maintenance update to its Cubit 13.2 mesh generation tool. The update provides more than two dozen improvements and fixes. csimsoft is committed to providing its customers with the highest quality products and to respond to their requests in a timely manner.

Customers with current rental agreements or maintenance programs can download the update from their csimsoft account.

csimsoft is a leading developer of pre-processing solutions for the computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis communities. (For more information, visit coreform.com)
