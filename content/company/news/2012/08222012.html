AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, August 22, 2012 — Thaker Simulation Technologies, a provider of computational simulation solutions, has joined csimsoft's Channel Partner Program as a North American distributor of csimsoft's geometry preparation and mesh generation software.

"Thaker Simulation Technologies is known for their ability to find viable CFD solutions for some of the world's top engineering firms," says Paul Ressler, csimsoft Director of Sales and Marketing. "We are very pleased to partner with Thaker as we develop our next generation of meshing tools. Their broad experience will help us meet the needs of our growing world-wide user base."

Thaker Simulation Technologies, LLC provides simulation based engineering services and software solutions to companies in various industries, including energy, chemical processing, and defense. (For more information, visit www.thakerllc.com)

csimsoft is a leading developer of pre-processing solutions for the computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis communities. (For more information, visit coreform.com)
