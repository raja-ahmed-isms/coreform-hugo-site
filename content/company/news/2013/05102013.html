AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, May 10, 2013 — Desktop Engineering, a premier technology publication for the engineering community, features csimsoft's Trelis in it's Check it Out section.

Editor Anthony Lockwood reviews Trelis software, highlighting its pedigree coming from CUBIT, a mesh generation tool that csimsoft developed in conjunction with Sandia National Laboratories. Mr. Lockwood identifies Trelis' powerful features and states, "Trelis is a high-end, yet general-purpose, mesh generation tool for nasty meshing jobs."

The article can be found on the Desktop Engineering website.
