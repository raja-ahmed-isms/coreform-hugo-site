AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, March 11, 2013 — csimsoft, a leading developer of pre-processing tools for CFD and FEA professionals, announced that it has partnered with Device Analytics of Carlsbad, CA. Device Analytics is a provider of engineering products and services with expertise in digital prototyping and simulation methods.

"Device Analytics offers a powerful combination of great engineering software tools and an experienced team of engineering professionals," said Paul Ressler, csimsoft's Director of Sales and Marketing. "They provide their clients with superior customer service and extremely reliable results."

Trelis 14.0 is based on the time-proven CUBIT™ code that has been developed for more than two decades in conjunction with Sandia National Laboratories and provides users with powerful meshing tools that specifically meet the needs of csimsoft's rapidly growing worldwide commercial user base.

About csimsoft

csimsoft is a leading developer of pre-processing solutions for the computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis communities. (For more information, visit csimsoft.com)

About Device Analytics LLC

Device Analytics specializes in the engineering of devices and components across multiple industries using FEA with probabilistic techniques to simulate realistic operating environments and to account for variations in manufacturing tolerances. (For more information, visit deviceanalytics.com/)
