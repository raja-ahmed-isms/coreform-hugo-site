AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, June 4, 2013 — csimsoft, a leading developer of advanced meshing tools for the CFD and FEA communities, announced today the release of Trelis 14.0.3, a maintenance release of its popular Trelis meshing software. Trelis 14.0.3 and a list of updates are available through the csimsoft website.

"This release is part of our ongoing commitment to provide best-in-class software to our rapidly growing global user base," said Randy Morris csimsoft's Managing Director. "Trelis 14.0.3 greatly improves the user experience and ensures the highest quality meshes."

Trelis 14 is based on the time-proven CUBIT™ code that has been developed for more than two decades in conjunction with Sandia National Laboratories and provides users with powerful meshing tools that specifically meet the needs of csimsoft's worldwide commercial user base.
