AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, May 11, 2015 — csimsoft, a leading developer of advanced meshing tools for the CFD and FEA, has released Trelis 15.1.4.

Trelis is based on the time-proven CUBIT™ code that has been developed for more than two decades by csimsoft in conjunction with Sandia National Laboratories. Trelis 15.1 provides powerful meshing tools that produce high-quality meshes and enhance the user experience.

Release notes are available at the csimsoft website.

About csimsoft

csimsoft is the developer of robust commercial-grade meshing sofware for challenging simulation for computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis. Delivering exceptional performance and outstanding value, csimsoft's award winning products are used by engineers who need precision in many of the world's largest organizations. csimsoft is headquartered in Utah, U.S.A.
