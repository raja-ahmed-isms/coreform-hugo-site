AMERICAN FORK, UTAH, May 16, 2015 — csimsoft announced today the release of version 15.2 of its Trelis mesh generation software for challenging engineering simulations.

"This version of Trelis is based on the suggestions from our growing number of customers across the globe." said Paul Ressler, Director of Sales and Marketing. "As hundreds more engineering organizations use Trelis we see new use cases for the software. 15.2 meets their need for increased performance, better interoperability, and greater ease-of-use."

Trelis 15.2 includes enhancements for meshing, geometry preparation, importing and exporting, and graphics. Release notes are available on the csimsoft website.

About csimsoft
